About Singapore insider disclosure

Regulator

Singapore Exchange (SGX) + Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS)

Disclosure deadline

2 business days

Legal framework

Securities and Futures Act — Part XII Sections 137 & 218

Coverage

STI 30 components (DBS, OCBC, UOB, SingTel, Keppel, CapitaLand…)

Singapore director dealings are filed as Form 1 (directors/CEOs) or Form 2 (substantial shareholders). Daily share buyback notices include structured data: exact shares purchased, high/low price, total consideration, and cumulative program progress.

What InsidersAlpha tracks for Singapore

InsidersAlpha collects SGX company announcements for STI 30 stocks via the SGX disclosure portal. This includes:

  • Director dealings (ANNC14): open-market purchases and sales by directors, CEOs, and substantial shareholders
  • Share buybacks (ANNC13): daily buyback execution reports with shares purchased, price range, and cumulative totals
  • Conviction scoring based on the insider's historical track record on the SGX
  • Signal detection: cluster buying, repetitive buying, price dip purchases

Coverage focuses on the 30 STI index components — Singapore's most liquid and actively-traded stocks including DBS Group, OCBC, UOB, Singapore Airlines, Keppel, Mapletree trusts, and Singapore Exchange itself.
