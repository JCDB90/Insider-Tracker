Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance — processing your email and subscription data to provide the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interest — usage analytics to improve the platform

Cookies

InsidersAlpha uses the following cookies:

  • Authentication session (Supabase) — stores your login session. Essential for the service to function. Expires when you sign out or after 7 days of inactivity.
  • Analytics (Google Analytics 4) — anonymous usage tracking. You can opt out via your browser settings or a GA opt-out extension.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party retargeting cookies.

Your rights under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to restrict processing — request restriction while a dispute is resolved

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@insidersalpha.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. On account deletion, personal data (email, subscription info) is deleted within 30 days. Anonymised analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the platform or by email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
